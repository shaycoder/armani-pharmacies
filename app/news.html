News & Events

Leadership Strengthens at Armani Pharmacy with Founder’s Vision and New Head Appointment

Armani Pharmacy continues to strengthen its leadership with the guidance of one of the most successful businessman and its founder - Dr. Shihab Sha, and the appointment of Mrs. Gaana Vijayan as Head. Dr. Shihab’s vision emphasizes innovation, accessibility and patient-focused pharmaceutical care, ensuring Armani Pharmacy remains a trusted name in the industry.

With Mrs. Gaana’s appointment, the pharmacy division is set to expand its services, introduce customer-friendly initiatives and enhance community-focused healthcare programs. Together, their leadership marks a significant step toward building a stronger, more innovative pharmacy network across the UAE.

Armani Pharmacy Expands with New Branch Openings

Armani Pharmacy is proud to announce the opening of new branches across the UAE, making high-quality medicines and healthcare products more accessible to communities. The expansion reflects Armani’s commitment to improving convenience for patients while maintaining excellence in customer care and trusted pharmaceutical services.

Launch of Digital Prescription & Home Delivery Service

In line with advancing patient convenience, Armani Pharmacy has launched its digital prescription and home delivery service. Customers can now upload their prescriptions online and receive medicines delivered directly to their doorstep. This innovative service ensures timely access to medication while promoting comfort and safety for patients.

Armani Pharmacy Hosts Free Health Awareness Campaign

Armani Pharmacy recently organized a community health awareness campaign, offering free blood pressure checks, diabetes screening and consultations with healthcare professionals. The initiative aimed to educate the public on preventive healthcare and encourage early detection of lifestyle-related conditions, reinforcing Armani Pharmacy’s role as a trusted partner in community well-being.
